## Overview
Welcome to the **Customer Support AI Agent** repository! This Python-based solution is designed to monitor product prices on an e-commerce site. By leveraging an ARIMA model, it predicts future prices and sends notifications to customers when the price of a product is expected to drop in the next 10 days.

## Features
üîç **Real-time Price Monitoring** - Keeps track of product prices on the e-commerce site in real-time.  
üîÆ **Price Prediction** - Utilizes an ARIMA model to predict future prices accurately.  
üìß **Notification System** - Notifies customers when the price of a product is expected to drop in the next 10 days.
